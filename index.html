Transforming Waste into Verifiable Assets.

EkoKred is an AI-driven platform creating a transparent, digital ecosystem to formalize India's waste economy, turning environmental liability into economic opportunity through verifiable plastic credits.

The Opportunity: A Market Shaped by Policy

India is at a pivotal moment, with a burgeoning waste crisis meeting a wave of progressive environmental policies. This creates a powerful, compliance-driven demand for innovative solutions that can bring efficiency, transparency, and value to the waste management sector.

India's Growing Waste Challenge

India's rapid urbanization is generating millions of tons of waste daily, creating immense environmental pressure. The government's "waste-to-wealth" vision is a direct call for platforms like EkoKred to build a circular economy.

Key Regulatory Tailwinds

A suite of new regulations, from Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) to a credit-based compliance system, are creating a mandatory market. Click on a policy to learn more.
